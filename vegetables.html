Growing Tomatoes

From planting to harvesting delicious tomatoes

Fresh tomatoes growing in a home garden

Planting Tips:

  • Soil: Well-draining soil with pH 6.0-6.8
  • Sunlight: 6-8 hours of direct sunlight daily
  • Spacing: 24-36 inches between plants
  • Watering: Consistent watering, avoid wetting leaves
  • Best Time: Plant after last frost in spring
  • Harvest: 60-80 days after planting

Common Tomato Varieties:

Cherry tomatoes, Beefsteak tomatoes, Roma tomatoes, Heirloom varieties

Vegetable Gardening Tips for Beginners

Starting a vegetable garden can be rewarding and productive. Begin with easy-to-grow vegetables like tomatoes, carrots, and lettuce. Ensure your garden gets at least 6 hours of sunlight daily and has well-draining soil. Regular watering and proper spacing are key to healthy plants. Container gardening is perfect for small spaces like balconies and patios.

Seasonal Planting Guide

Spring: Lettuce, carrots, peas | Summer: Tomatoes, peppers, cucumbers | Fall: Kale, spinach, radishes | Winter: Indoor herbs, microgreens
